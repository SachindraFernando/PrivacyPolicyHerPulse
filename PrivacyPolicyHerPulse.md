Privacy Policy for HerPulse

Last updated: March 30, 2026

HerPulse is designed with your privacy as the top priority.

Data Collection: HerPulse does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter (cycle dates, symptoms, moods, notes) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Analytics: HerPulse does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking tools. We have zero visibility into how you use the app.

Third-Party Services: HerPulse does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Data Sharing: Your data is never shared with anyone. Period.

Data Export: You may choose to export a PDF report via the built-in share sheet. This is entirely user-initiated and HerPulse has no access to where you send it.

Data Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time from Settings > Delete All My Data.
